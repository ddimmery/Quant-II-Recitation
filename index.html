This is the Course Website for the recitation for Quant II.

The TA is Drew Dimmery (which is why this is on his github). Recitation meets at 11am on Fridays for one hour. His office hours are 1 to 2 and 4 to 5 on Tuesdays, in office 317 at 19 W. 4th St.
